Calculator inputs

Most calculator inputs are processed in your browser to generate immediate results. We do not ask users to create an account for normal calculator use. You should still avoid entering highly sensitive personal, financial, medical, or confidential information into public online tools.

Contact messages

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and the information you choose to include. We use that information to understand your request, reply when appropriate, and improve the website.

Policy updates

We may update this privacy policy when the website, tools, analytics, or advertising setup changes. The latest version is published on this page. For privacy questions, contact support.aiagents@gmail.com.
