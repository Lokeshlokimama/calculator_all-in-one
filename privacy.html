Privacy Policy

Privacy information for calculator users.

This policy explains how Calculator All-in-One handles basic website data, advertising, analytics, and contact messages.

Privacy PolicyLast updated: 28 June 2026Questions: support.aiagents@gmail.com

Calculator inputs

Most calculator arithmetic is processed in your browser and is not submitted to Calculator All-in-One. No account is required for normal use. Do not enter confidential financial, medical, identity, authentication, or legal information into public online tools.

Inputs sent to third parties

The currency converter sends the selected base currency to open.er-api.com to request indicative rates. The QR generator sends the text or URL you enter to api.qrserver.com to create the QR image. Those providers receive the request and your IP address under their own privacy practices. Do not use the QR tool for passwords, payment credentials, private records, or authentication tokens.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is used to understand aggregate visits and page usage. Depending on browser settings, consent choices, and Google configuration, it may receive device and browser information, approximate location derived from IP address, page addresses, referring pages, and interaction events. We use this information to identify errors and improve navigation and content.

Google AdSense

Google AdSense supports free access to the site. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP address, device identifiers, and similar technologies to deliver, measure, limit, or personalize advertising where permitted. Ads are separate from calculator results and we never ask visitors to click them.

Cookies and privacy controls

Your browser can block or delete cookies. Where required, a consent message should provide choices before optional advertising or analytics storage is used. You can review Google advertising controls at My Ad Center and Google\'s data explanation at How Google uses information from partner sites.

Contact messages

If you email us, we receive the address and content you choose to send. We use it to respond, reproduce a reported problem, consider a correction, and improve the site. Do not email passwords, financial-account details, government identifiers, medical records, or other sensitive data.

External links and retention

External sites have their own policies. Analytics, advertising, exchange-rate, QR, email, hosting, and payment providers determine their own retention periods. Calculator All-in-One does not maintain user accounts or a server-side history of ordinary calculator inputs.

Children and professional decisions

The site is a general-audience utility and is not intended to collect personal information from children. Health, finance, tax, legal, and engineering outputs are educational estimates; users should verify important decisions with qualified professionals and current official sources.

Policy changes and contact

This policy is updated when data handling, providers, advertising, analytics, or calculator behavior changes. For privacy questions or deletion requests concerning information sent directly by email, contact support.aiagents@gmail.com and describe the relevant message.
