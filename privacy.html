Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account to use the tools. Most calculator inputs are processed in your browser and are not intentionally stored by us.

If you contact us by email, we receive the email address and information you choose to send.

Browser Storage

The site may use browser storage for convenience, such as caching currency-rate data, remembering rotating tool cards, or improving the local experience.

You can clear this data from your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

The site may connect to services such as Google Fonts, ExchangeRate-API for currency rates, and Razorpay for voluntary support payments.

Payment details are handled by Razorpay. Do not send card, UPI, password, or bank information to us directly.

How We Use Information

We use contact messages to respond to feedback, fix bugs, and improve the tools. Support helps maintain hosting/API costs and ongoing platform maintenance.

We do not sell personal information that you send to us.
