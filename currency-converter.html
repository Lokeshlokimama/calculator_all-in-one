What this currency converter does

This currency converter helps you estimate the value of one currency in another currency. It is useful for travel planning, freelance invoices, international shopping, tuition payments, online subscriptions, savings comparisons, and checking approximate remittance values. Enter the amount, choose the source currency, choose the target currency, and convert.

The tool requests live exchange rate data when you calculate. Exchange rates change throughout the day as markets move, and the rate used by a bank, card network, wallet, or money transfer provider may include markup, fees, or a different update time. That is why the result should be treated as an estimate rather than a guaranteed transaction rate.

For practical use, compare the converted amount with fees and the final rate shown by your provider before paying. If you are planning a trip, it can help you build a budget. If you are receiving international payments, it can help you understand approximate local value before the payment lands. For accounting or tax filing, keep official statements and use the exchange rate rules required by your jurisdiction.

Currency conversion formula

Converted amount = source amount x exchange rate. The exchange rate tells how much one unit of the source currency is worth in the target currency.

Example calculation

If 1 USD = 83 INR, then 100 USD = 100 x 83 = 8,300 INR before fees or provider markup.

FAQs

Are the rates live?

The converter fetches live rate data when you run a conversion, subject to provider availability.

Will my bank use the same rate?

Not always. Banks and cards may add fees, spreads, or use a different rate timing.

Can I use this for invoices?

Yes for estimates, but confirm the exact rate and date required by your invoice or contract.

Why did conversion fail?

The live rate provider may be temporarily unavailable or your connection may be offline.
