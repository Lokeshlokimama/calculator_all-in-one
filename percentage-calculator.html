What this percentage calculator does

Percentages are used everywhere: exam marks, discount labels, GST, interest rates, profit margins, salary hikes, battery level, attendance, analytics, and business reports. This calculator answers the common question: what is X percent of Y? Enter the percentage in the first field and the base value in the second field to get the result instantly.

A percentage is a fraction out of 100. When you say 20%, it means 20 parts for every 100 parts. To find 20% of 500, the calculator converts 20% into 0.20 and multiplies it by 500. The result is 100. This simple idea helps you compare values of different sizes because percentages put them on the same scale.

This page is useful for fast everyday checks, but it is also helpful when explaining calculations to students or clients. For example, a shop discount, a school score, a service charge, or a tax estimate becomes easier to verify when the formula is visible. If you need percentage increase, decrease, or detailed finance calculations, use the related calculators linked below.

Percentage formula

Result = (percentage / 100) x value.

Example calculation

To find 18% of 2,500: result = 18 / 100 x 2,500 = 450. So 18% of 2,500 is 450.

FAQs

What does percent mean?

Percent means per hundred. For example, 25% means 25 out of 100.

Can I use decimals?

Yes. You can enter values like 12.5% or 999.99.

Is this useful for discounts?

Yes. Use it to find the discount amount, then subtract it from the original price.

Can this calculate GST?
