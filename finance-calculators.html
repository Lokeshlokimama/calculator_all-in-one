Borrowing and repayment

Use the EMI Calculator or Loan Calculator to estimate a fixed monthly payment, total repayment and total interest. Compare loans using the same principal and term, then add processing fees, insurance and taxes from the lender's official quotation. A smaller monthly payment can result from a longer term while costing more interest overall.

Saving and investing

The SIP Calculator projects monthly contributions under a constant return assumption. The homepage also includes fixed-deposit and recurring-deposit tools. Investment returns are not guaranteed, so test conservative and optimistic rates and consider inflation, fees, tax, market volatility and contribution timing before treating a projection as a goal.

Tax, prices and pay

Use the GST Calculator to add or remove tax from a price, the Percentage Calculator for discounts and portions, and the salary or leave-encashment tools for simple pay estimates. Current tax classification, exemptions, payroll rules and invoice rounding must be checked with official documents.

Currency and travel budgets

The Currency Converter uses an available reference rate to estimate a destination amount. A card issuer, bank, cash exchange or transfer service may apply a spread, fee, tax or different settlement-time rate. Always compare three-letter currency codes as well as symbols and obtain a final provider quote before sending money.

Check a finance result before acting

First confirm units: annual versus monthly rate, years versus months, and tax-inclusive versus tax-exclusive price. Next reproduce the result with a simple boundary case, such as a zero-interest loan where principal divided by months should equal the payment. Finally, identify what the model excludes. Banks can use different payment dates and rounding; investment platforms can charge fees; tax authorities can change rates; and currency providers can quote a retail rate different from a reference feed. Use these calculators to compare scenarios, not as a binding offer, tax filing, investment recommendation or guarantee.
